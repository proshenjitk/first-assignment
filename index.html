Recipe One

Wash mutton pieces with salt, 1 tsp turmeric and ⅔ tsp lemon juice. Mix well and keep it for an hour. Heat mustard oil in a pressure cooker, add mutton pieces to it and cook on medium-high heat. This is to remove excess water from meat. When done remove the meat and keep aside.
